## About Me

I'm a Data Analyst who believes that **data is only as valuable as the decisions it enables.**

My journey started with a curiosity about why businesses succeed or fail — and I quickly realized the answer almost always lives inside the data. Since then I've been focused on one question: *how do you take thousands of rows of raw, messy data and turn them into something a business can actually act on?*

That's what I do. I own the full analytics pipeline — from ingesting raw files and cleaning dirty data, all the way to building dashboards and delivering insights that drive real strategy.

What excites me most right now is how AI is transforming the way we work with data. Tools like machine learning, natural language processing, and AI-assisted analytics are making it possible to process larger datasets faster, detect patterns that would take humans weeks to find, and reduce human error in the cleaning and transformation process. I actively integrate AI tools into my workflow — not to replace analytical thinking, but to sharpen it. AI handles the noise so I can focus on the meaning.

I bring this mindset across every industry I've worked in — from engineering services and workforce analytics to apparel, e-commerce, and business development.
